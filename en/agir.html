Act for the environment
Discover below some tricks to act for the environment daily
Buy in bulk
A way to drastically reduce your ecological impact is to reduce waste production. Use less packaging can help reduce pollution due to manufacturing, the transport and disposal of this waste. It is therefore preferable to buy in bulk rather than in packages.
Prioritize public transport and carpooling
Transport, particularly cars, emits the most emissions of greenhouse gas. To reduce this emission, alternatives exist such as public transport or carpooling. These techniques make possible the reduction of the emission of greenhouse gases per person
Manage your heating
Heating requires a lot of energy. To reduce its ecological impact, it is important to properly manage its heating. It is preferable to use renewable energies and to properly insulate your home to limit energy losses.
