Climate fresco - 26 and 28 september 2023

“Climate fresco” workshops intended for students at the IUT du Littoral were
made. Two workshops were held from 8:30 a.m. and 11:45 a.m. on Tuesday 26 and Thursday 28 to
raise awareness among students.

Waste collection - 10 October 2023

Unfortunately, we find more and more waste in the nature. To help protecting nature
a waste collection is organized on Dunkerque beach at 11am.

Conference “The worst of greenwashing” - 30 October 2023

The "greenwashing" is a deceptive practice used by some enterprise to make believe to the public
that they are respectful with environment, but in reality they only take superficials measures. Discoverd the
worth of “greenwashing” during a conference at the Descarte amphitheater in the IUT of Saint Omer at 2pm.

Conference “Resist the air lobby“ - 16 February 2024

The airplane, a way of transport very used for intercontinental transport but also very polluting.
Discover all the disadvantages of using this mode of transport during the conference
“From Notre Dame des Landes to Montreal, resist the air lobby” at 7 pm at the Gaumont cinema in Lille.
